Dog Breeds A to Z

The most comprehensive Dog Breed app available!

All dog breeds are avaliable from go, no aditional in app purchases or advertising like others. 1000s of glossy photos.

We put together an app which contains all the essential facts covering just short of 200 breeds of dog with many more to follow soon!!.

This app has been created with dog ownership for families in mind so includes the main characteristics you need to know about dogs from all over the world in handy reference app.

Each dog breed includes many glossy full screen photos, description of typical characteristics and temperaments, details of origins, family suitability, average breed size and weight, health facts and many others.

Anyone contemplating getting a dog need not come any further. Do a little homework to understand the likely demands of different breed types before making the life changing commitment.

See a dog in the street you like, see instant info, great for the kids with thousands of pictures for them to see. Same app works on both iPhone and IPad.

The App will be continue updated with new breeds and information. If you do not see a breed you are interested in, let us know and we will add it!
